Key Challenges Farmers Face

1. Water Management

Many farmers struggle with water shortages and inefficient irrigation methods.

2. Climate Change

Unpredictable weather conditions affect crop yields and farming schedules.

3. Soil Health

Over-farming and lack of nutrients reduce soil fertility over time.

4. Market Access

Many small-scale farmers find it hard to sell their produce at fair prices.
